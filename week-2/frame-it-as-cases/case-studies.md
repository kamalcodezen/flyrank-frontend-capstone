# Case Study 1: Prompting Fundamentals (FL-02)

## The Problem

When I started this assignment, my biggest challenge was understanding English prompts. I could read English, but I often struggled to understand the actual meaning and intent. Because of that, I found it difficult to write effective prompts. During the Agentic AI project, I realized that weak prompts led to poor AI responses, and I often got stuck because I didn't know how to guide the AI properly.

## What I Did

Instead of changing everything at once, I improved my prompts one step at a time. After every change, I compared the responses from Claude and ChatGPT to understand what improved and why. This helped me identify which prompt changes actually made a difference.

## The Outcome

After completing this assignment, I became much more confident in writing prompts. I learned how to improve prompts step by step and better understand English prompts. I can now understand Claude's responses more easily and communicate my ideas to AI much more effectively.

# Case Study 2: The Prompt Ladder (FL-03)

## The Problem

I used to change many things in a prompt at once, so I could not understand which change actually improved the AI's response.

## What I Did

I improved the prompt one layer at a time. After every version, I compared the output with the previous version and noted what improved, what still failed, and what I should try next.

## The Outcome

This assignment taught me how to evaluate prompts systematically. Instead of guessing, I now understand which prompt changes improve the quality of AI responses and why.
